Low Explosives

We will talk about obtaining three types of low explosives. These are blackpowder, flash powder and nitrocellulose.
Black powder is the easiet to obtain and is the safest to work with that's why I personaly prefer this. It can be extratced from most firecrackers and made easily in home by combinig salt petre, charcoal and sulphur in 70:20:10 ratio and then adding water and blending it in a mixer grinder.
Then dry it and properly grind it to make a fine powder.
Flash Powder is not possible to make at home easily but you can extract it from firecrackers like cherry bombs, m-80s and showers but it's very risky to use because of it's sensitive nature.
It's so dangerous that a little bit of friction or heat can ignite it.
I will advise not to use it. Talking about nitrocellulose, it can be made using nitrating cotton with nitration acid but it's expensive to produce and it inloves a lot of chemistry so you must watch a Youtube video by a respected chemistry teacher.
I would recommended this video: Nitrocellulose
In truth, all of these are dangerous to work with. So, be very careful when handling.
Lastly, blackpowder though mostly 'safe' is much weaker and may not always produce the expected results.

Homemade Naplam

This is very simple, just mix polystyrene or expanded polystyrene (thermocol ) with gasoline and stir it.
But you can also use diesel or kerosene instead of gasoline.
The ultimate result is a thick white liquid which burns at very high tempretures and does'nt easily vaporizes like gasoline, diesel or kerosene when burned.
But this mixture when burned releases toxic fumes, so be careful.
Make sure to wear gloves, goggles and mask for safety and keep it away from even the slightest bit of heat because it's highly flammable.
Also have an extinguisher nearby or atleat sand or water.

Molotov Cocktail

Invented during the winter war, molotovs are very easy and cheap to make weapons, popular among rebels and criminals.
To make it, fill a glass bottle which will break easily on impact (like liquor bottles) with the homemade naplam alternative.
Then just stuff a fuse into it, which is made by soaking cotton rag in gasoline or diesel or kerosene.
Gasoline is the popular choice. Keep it away from fire.

Pipe Bomb

This can be assambled in various ways and that's why ingridients varies a lot but I am going to list my prefered method here.
First we need a small cutting of a metal pipe then low explosives and lastly threaded caps.
First cut some threads on one opening of your pipe and put the threaded cap.
This cap must have a drilled hole because you will be putting a visco fuse there.
Then on another end weld a threaded cap.
Now you have created a bottle like container.
Now fill it with the low explosives and add the visco fuse after closing the drilled threaded cap.
You can also add duct tape to reinforce the openings.
You can also connect a igniter to the fuse too.
Now your pipe bomb is ready.

Pressure Cooker Bomb

This is the most dangerous item in this guide and it's notorious for being used in terrorist attacks.
This one may seem a little easy to make but it's really diffuclt too guarntee safety and successful explosion.
Obviously we need a pressure cooker first.
Then we will fill it with low explosives and sharpnels.
There are many options for the sharpnels, like nails, screws, bearings, hex nuts etc. but my suggestion is nails, bearings and razor blades.
Add nails the most and razor blades the least and bearnings moderately.
Be carefull about not adding too much sharpnel as it will lower the amount of explosives and therefore pressure, and the pressure cooker will produce most of the sharpnels during the explosion anyway.
Now put the lid on add epoxy before doing it.
There must be a small drilled hole in the lid to put fuse.
We have to remove the pressure valve and block the steam releasing hole with epoxy and duct tape.
Finally reinforce the whole lid with duct tape.
Now the sealing is complete.
Be carefull not to ever use welding to seal the lid as the low explosives will certainly ignite.
Now put a visco fuse from the hole and your pressure cooker bomb is ready.
You can add a igniter which is recommended or just ignite the fuse with a lighter.

Bottle Bomb

This is a special creation by me as an alternative to pipe bomb.
Take a big steel bottle with a drilled cap, fill it with low explosives and sharpnels.
In this case screws, hex nuts and bearnigs are best as they take relatively less space than nails and razor blades and a bottle is much smaller than a pressure cooker.
Put the cap with epoxy and reinforce with duct tape and a fuse and maybe a igniter and it's ready.

Chlorine Bomb

This is the closest thing to a chemical weapon delivary system you can make at home.
This thing is also called an Acid Bomb because acid being an important ingridient.
Though not all acid bombs release chlorine.
Chlorine is a chemical weapon widely used in the First World War.
It is a respiratory agent, which means it attacks lungs.
It reacts with the moistute of your eyes and lungs to form hydrochloric acid.
To make this chlorine bomb you need an empty fraglie glass bottle or an empty packaged drinking water bottle so it easily ruptures from gas pressure.
First take bleach (sodium hypochlorite) and hydrochloric acid in 2:1 mole ratio in the bottle and quickly put the cap on and throw the bottle.
It will form chlorine gas inside which will create pressure and the bottle will rupture, releasing chlorine gas.
If you used a glass bottle or put sharpnels (like nails) inside the bottle, it would make it even more deadly as upon rupture sharpnels and glass fragments will spread outward.
This must be done quickly otherwise it will explode in your hands.
This weapon is extremly dangerous to use so be very very careful.
Also when mixing chemicals, make sure to wear mask, goggles and gloves. There is another version of chlorine bomb though which uses chlorine tablets and other reagents.

Alkali-Oil Bomb

To make this you need a plastic or glass container like a jar or bottle then put water and sharpnels like nails, bearings inside.
After that put gasoline, diesel or kerosene then add pieces of sodium or potassium on the oil.
Now the oil layer will seprate the alkali metals from water so reaction between alkali and water doesn't happen. But when you will throw this the alkali will go into the water and the bottle will explode.
Remember to use this immediatly as it will instantly explode if the bottle falls and always keep the bottle standing so alkali doesn't go into the water.
