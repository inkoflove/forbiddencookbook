Nitroglycerin

In this section we will not only talk about nitroglycerin but other explosives based on it, specifically blasting gelatin and dynamite.
In summary nitroglycerin can be made in home by nitrating glycerine with nitration acid. Blasting gelatin is a mixture of nitrocellulose and nitroglycerin and dynamite is nitroglycerin soaked into a porus material such as starch or silica.
Nitroglycerin is extremely sensitive and dangerous but blasting gelatin and dynamite is much safer, both being invented by Alfred Nobel.
Nitroglycerin is not possible to produce at home in high amounts without turning it into a factory with expert workers and safety equipment.
That's why in my previous bomb making recipes, I have always talked about using low explosives because you can't make enough of them to use in bombs.
In this recipe we will talk about making low amounts of nitroglycerin and so others.
As nitroglycerin is complex to make, I recommend you to watch this:Nitroglycerine.
Now let's talk about making blasting gelatin. It's not actual gelatin. It's just looks like it. You need dissolve 7 to 13% of the weight of the nitroglycerin worth of nitrocellulose in the nitroglycerin and slowly and carefully stir it.
Your blasting gelatin is ready but to properly fo this process you must have prior chemistry knowledge and experience working with sensitive chemicals.
Next we will be talking about a simple method of making dynamite.
This is more easier to execute but may produce a weaker dynamite.
You need around 30% nitroglycerin, and 70% starch (percentage by weight) and then mix them slowly and you get dynamite.
All of these needs a lab, proper safety gear and prior experience and knowledge of chemistry to make.

Mercury Fulminate

Mercury Fulminate with the chemical formula of Hg(CNO)2 is a highly sensitive and toxic primary high explosive.
It was used mainly for making primers and blasting caps.
It's sensitivity and easy degradation over time made it obsolete.

Picric Acid

Picric acid (2,4,6-trinitrophenol) is a yellow, crystalline organic compound used used primarily in laboratory staining, synthesis, and historically as a high explosive.
While stable when wet,dry picric acid is highly friction- and shock-sensitive explosive, forming unstable metal salts too.

Dry Ice Bomb

Dry Ice Bombs are made by putting dry ice and water in a plastic container, for example a plastic bottle.
First get a big plastic container (atleast a 2L bottle) then fill the half of the bottle with water and then add lots of dry ice and close the bottle.
After some time the bottle will explode.
This happens because in water the dry ice subliminates and produces carbon dioxide which creates extreme pressure on the container and it explodes.
If you added some sharpnels in the water too, that would be deadly.
Dry Ice Bomb in general is a unpredictable and dangerous to use device and can cause serious injuries even to the user.

Handmade Grenade

Handmade Grenade can be made using a can like of condensed milk or similar cans of canned foods.
The can is obviously open so first weld it then drill a hole and attach a funnel to it.
After that funnel low explosives into it and also add shrapnels like nails, razor blades, bearing etc from the hole.
My recommended combination is same as the pressure cooker bomb shrapnel combination.
Finally add a visco fuse to the hole and seal the with lots duct tape but the fuse must remain outside.
You may attach a stick to it with tape for better range.
If you only use nails as shrapnels it would be a nail bomb too.
But remember,the welding must be done before putting low explosives in the can.

Tear Gas Grenade

This is also my creation.
To make this fill a emptied food can with fragments of tire, homemade naplam and two spoons of pottasium nitrate.
Then seal the can with duct tape but remember to drill a hole for a visco fuse.
We are not making a handmade grenade so we didn't do welding on the can and only used duct tape for sealing but it's not enough.
We need to drill a big hole or multiple small holes on the can and cover it with a duct tape.
Before igniting the fuse you must remove the tape as it will release smoke and prevent building pressure and use a insulating tape because it's easy to remove.
This device releases both gas and smoke unlike regular tear gas.
Regular tear gas is a chemical called CS but this device releases sulphur dioxide, carbon monoxide, styrene, PAHs, VOCs, benzene, carbon dioxide and toxic particulate matter containing black soot and heavy metals.
It will irritate eyes and lungs and cause nausea, coughing, headache, dizziness.
This is much more toxic than regular tear gas.
This gases can cause permanent health issues if breated in high amounts.
Overall it works as a more toxic and crude version of regular tear gas.

Homemade Wired Igniter

This is much easier to make than a RC Igniter.
First we need to create a open circuit with car battery, wires and a switch.
Now join the end of wires and a visco fuse together while the switch is off.
It's ready. Turn on the switch and the fuse will be ignited.
